Here at Weightless Ltd. it is our passion to deliver the very best in personal and business cloud services to our customers. Our goal is simple: deliver reliable and secure storage to all our customers. This allows them more time to pursue the causes that interest them, whilst removing the stress, confusion and (most importantly) space associated with physical storage that has palgued businesses for decades.

For over 10 years, Weightless Ltd. has provided consumers with easy and reliable access to cloud networks and expert customer services. Founded in 2006, just after the Amazon's revolutionary launch of the Elastic Compute cloud (EC2), we've grown from strength-to-strength and now become a global leader in all there is to offer on cloud computing.
